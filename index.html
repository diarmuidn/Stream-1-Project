What We're About short answer: Cars

Long Answer

Here at Carpedia we exist to give you the motorist a fairer deal on your car repairs and maintenance. With an ever expanding database of cars whatever issue your car faces we use our constantly evolving fairpriceguide to lead you every step of the way in your dealings with mechanics and car dealerships.

Why us?

Quite simply because there exists no other organistaion or service whose sole purpose is to remove the arbitary pricing of car related problems. From our humble origins we have shaken the wider industry into action and brought fairer pricing for everybody.

The future

The Carpedia community is the future of our service. This is where you the car owner can interact with millions of motorists across the world regarding any motor related issue. Whether it's looking for a spare part for your 1970 VW Beetle or a picturesque driving route along the French Riveria the Carpedia community really is your one-stop shop for all things car related.
