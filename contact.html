  1. Carpedia's Birthday

    January 16th, 2014

    Carpedia was initially conceived as a newsletter with the simple goal of aiding people with any car related issues in our local area of Co Clare Ireland.

  2. Going Live

    February 28th, 2014

    It was just over one year later after months of painstaking work that our first beta website went live.

  3. Founding of our fairpriceguide

    March 20th, 2014

    The fairpriceguide is essentially the fruit of our global database. It allows us to crunch the numbers bringing you the consumer a more fluid experience.

  4. Our first foray outside Ireland

    May 20th, 2014

    The initial success and consumer demand for more information regarding cars far and beyond just Ireland meant we created a template which could be applied internationally.

  5. Opening of our Dublin office

    July 9th, 2014

    With an ever expanding skilled labour pool demand we decided upon a permanent abode in our nations capital as the ideal place for Carpedia's HQ.

  6. Over 100 employees

    August 30th, 2014

    Starting out as a team of two alternating between bedrooms and garages it was a momentus occassion to reach the milestone of employing over 100 professionals whom we are deeply invested in.

  7. 100 Million App Downloads

    September 15th, 2014

    An incredible journey from such humble origins. This company was about helping people in its infancy, today this ethos remains. We are constantly innovating and the search for perfection shall continue in earnest.
